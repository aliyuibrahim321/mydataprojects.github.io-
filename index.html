Unilever canteen ordering system projects

This project centered around the development and implementation of an innovative canteen ordering system. Unilever, a British-Dutch multinational FMCG company headquartered in London, is one of the oldest in the sector, with products distributed in nearly 190 countries. At its UK offices, which span 12 floors, Unilever employs about 1,500 people, supported by two canteens, each with a capacity of 150 diners at a time. The goal of the project was to improve the lunch experience by introducing a web-based ordering system. This system allows employees to pre-order their meals online, reducing wait times and cutting down on food waste. Key features of the system include online ordering, menu management, order processing, a delivery service, payment integration, and a feedback mechanism. The platform is designed to boost employee satisfaction, streamline operations, and reduce the environmental footprint of food waste using microsoft excel.
